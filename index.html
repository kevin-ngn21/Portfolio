About Me

I am a recent Marketing graduate from the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, I bring a mix of hands-on experience, creative problem-solving, and innovative thinking to every project I manage, as well as a sharp eye for detail, and the ability to excel independently and collaboratively.

I love combining creativity with analytics to build campaigns that resonate and deliver results. This site showcases the projects and campaigns I’ve worked on throughout my journey.

Featured Projects

Petro-Canada Associate Supplier Program

In this role, I managed the Associate Supplier Program, supporting promotional content for Petro-Canada stations nationwide. I coordinated briefings, creative development, and proofing with agencies to ensure brand alignment, clear communication, and timely delivery of marketing materials while collaborating with cross-functional teams to implement feedback and execute campaigns smoothly.

Petro-Canada: 2023 Fall Campaign

Worked on social media promotions that highlights Petro-Canada's newest deals during the Fall season of 2023. These initiatives aimed to enhance brand engagement and drive increased traffic to every Petro-Canada station by aligning with seasonal trends and customers' wants. Responsibilities for this project include developing ideas for the content used and maintaining communications with Petro-Canada's digital marketing agency. ​

Petro-Canada: 2023 Halloween Campaign

Worked on a Halloween promotion for Petro-Canada that displays the various things you can use to make a makeshift Halloween costume using Petro-Canada products. Responsibilities for this project include communication coordination between Petro-Canada's designated marketing agency and Petro-Canada's Marketing Lead, and content creation development.​
